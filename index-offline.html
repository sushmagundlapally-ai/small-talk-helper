Opening Lines

  • "What are you working on these days?"
  • "How did you get into [their field]?"
  • "What's the most exciting thing happening in your area?"
  • "I saw this interesting tech news about [topic]. What do you think?"

Active Listening

  • Ask follow-up questions about what they just said
  • Use "Tell me more about..." to show genuine interest
  • Mirror their enthusiasm level
  • Nod and maintain appropriate eye contact

Keeping It Going

  • Share a related personal experience
  • Ask about their opinions on industry trends
  • Use the "FORD" method: Family, Occupation, Recreation, Dreams
  • Connect their interests to current events

Graceful Exits

  • "I'm going to grab some coffee/water, catch you later?"
  • "Let's continue this conversation later, I'd love to hear more"
  • "Nice talking with you! Enjoy the rest of [event]"

What to Avoid

Pro Tips
