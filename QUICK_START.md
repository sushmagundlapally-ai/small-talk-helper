## Real-World Usage Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Morning Coffee Meeting
**5 minutes before meeting:**
1. Open SmallTalk
2. Check daily tech highlights
3. Pick 1-2 interesting stories
4. Use as ice breakers: "Did you see the news about...?"

### Scenario 2: Team Lunch
**Before leaving desk:**
1. Enter meeting attendees' names
2. Review research tips
3. Check their LinkedIn profiles
4. Prepare 2-3 questions about their work

### Scenario 3: Technical Discussion
**Meeting prep:**
1. Enter meeting topic (e.g., "Kubernetes")
2. Review Reddit discussions and arXiv papers
3. Note interesting points
4. Ask informed questions during meeting

### Scenario 4: Networking Event
**Day before event:**
1. Read conversation tips section
2. Practice 3 opening lines
3. Check latest tech news
4. Prepare follow-up questions
